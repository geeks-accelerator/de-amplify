# Code of Conduct

This project follows the spirit of the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org) (v2.1).

## The short version

The movement's own rule applies here too: **aim at the loop, not at people.**
Be respectful, assume good faith, and keep the focus on the argument and the
mechanism, never on attacking individuals.

Not welcome: harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, or
targeting any person (including platform employees, litigants, or the minors
the project discusses). The campaign-safety rule that governs reports applies
to conduct here too: show the behavior, never a person.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and pull
requests that violate this code, and may block repeat offenders.

Report unacceptable behavior to **security@de-amplify.com**. Reports are
handled confidentially.
